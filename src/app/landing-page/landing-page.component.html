Welcome to BibOnline

Your online and powerful college library management system

About us

BibOnline represents an online college library management system which comes in your help to ensure a good quality in the management of all articles available in your college, such as books, scientific articles, CDs and DVDs.

With the help of BibOnline you can ensure for your students the possibilty to borrow different articles from your library and manage easily their situations like statuses of the articles, the time until the item is returned, taxes for exceeding the borrow deadline. Also, as a librarian, you can easily manage the amount of your articles every day, from every device, from every browser, this library management system adapting a responsive platform.

Features

Articles management

As it was mentioned in description, as a librarian you can handle easily the stocks of your availabale articles with the help of some clicks.

Students database management

You can ensure the access for your students which are enrolled to your school. This feature can be done only by the administrators of the library.

Loan a lot of articles

We didn't forget you, students! You will have the possibility to loan different articles registered by your librarians and verify the statuses or taxes after deadline.

Request articles

Your librarian has not an very desired article? No problem! As a student, you will have the possibility to request some articles and these requests will be treated as soon as possible by your librarian.

Anytime, anywhere

As soon as you have an account to this platform, even if we talk about librarians or students, you can have everywhere, at every hour, access to our management system.

Responsive application

In a world full of technology, a mandatory requirement is that to ensure an easy accesibility for every type of device. Happily, this management system answers affirmatively at this check.
